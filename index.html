Little Frog on a Lily Pad

@nonbinarycowboy

Before I do anything, I ask myself: “Is this something a little frog on a lily pad would do?”

And if the answer is “no,” I’m busy that day. Can’t be there.

If I wake up and say “Gee, I think I want to wear a little vest today. I want to
play the banjo by the riverbank.” I’m free to do that.

That’s well within my rights!

But if I woke up and said, “Today I want to be unkind to the candlestick maker.”

You’re freaking kidding yourself if you think a little frog would do that.
